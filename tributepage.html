Albert Einstein

Scientist

1879-1955

He became a German citizen in 1914 and remained in Berlin until 1933 when he renounced his citizenship
for political reasons and emigrated to America to take the position of Professor
of Theoretical Physics at Princeton*. He became a United States citizen in 1940 and retired from his post in 1945.
